Unicode

You know how we use computers, mobiles, and tablets all the time? Well, those devices don't actually understand letters and words like we do. Instead, they only understand numbers. So, to solve this problem, people created something called an "encode" that matches each letter to a number. But, not all letters and characters were included in these encodes, so different encodes were used in different countries. That caused some confusion and problems. That's why they came up with a better solution called Unicode.

Unicode has a ton of characters and letters from all over the world, so now we can read and write in any language we want! It's important for our devices and is used everywhere. Cool, right?
